An evil organization is trying to master the power of the symbiote and create a new, more dangerous variant. Based on the short clip, the story opens with Eddie and Venom stuck to a plane in flight. They are being hunted by a large alien from Venom's home planet. On the other hand, they are also currently being hunted by government agents.
